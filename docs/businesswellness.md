At Fifty Firsts Wellness, we help businesses build wellbeing into the everyday, building a foundation of performance and belonging; not a perk, but as a strategic advantage.

We know that when people feel supported, connected and empowered, they show up differently. They’re more engaged, more resilient and more likely to thrive. That’s why our workplace wellness services are designed to reduce absence, manage stress, prevent burnout, create open discussions on mental health and menopause, and foster a culture where wellbeing isn’t just talked about or an afterthought, but an integral strategy, embedded into everything you do to optimise employee experience and performance. 

What We Offer
We work with businesses to co-create wellness solutions that are:

Strategic: aligned with your values, culture, and business goals
Scalable: built to grow with your team and adapt to change
Inclusive: designed for frontline staff, managers, and everyone in between
Actionable:  easy to implement, measure, and sustain
Our services, which will support your compliance with the Employments Rights Bill, include:

Menopause Policy, Strategy and Action Plans: tailored support, policy guidance and training for teams
Policy and Strategy Development (or Refresh): Your wellness partner to develop or refresh your Workplace wellbeing programme with policy, strategy, and planning
Wellbeing Ambassador Training: frameworks and onboarding for peer-led wellbeing ambassadors; sessions to help you embed wellbeing and educate those passionate about supporting others (certificate awarded)
Workplace Needs Assessments: collaborative audits / needs assessments to identify gaps and opportunities
Bespoke Wellbeing Training: Workshops tailored to support your policy, strategy and equip your team with necessary knowledge and awareness
Branded Content & Toolkits: engaging resources for internal comms and campaigns
Workshops & Programmes: Open and transparent sessions on midlife transitions, supporting an aging / diverse workforce, mental health and more
Workplace Space and Office Planning: Helping you to co-create a welcoming office environment that fosters physical, mental and social wellness, including tech free zones and quiet spaces. Working with designers, fitters and architects as needed
Bespoke Services: Working with therapists, nutritionists, financial and cyber wellbeing partners (and much more) we can create bespoke wellness support to suit your needs.
